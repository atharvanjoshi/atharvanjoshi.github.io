About Me

Hi, I'm Atharva—an AI Technical Lead and Senior Data Scientist based in London. With a strong focus on highly regulated domains like Trader Surveillance and Financial Misconduct Detection, I build scalable AI systems that transform how enterprises process complex, unstructured data at scale. Currently working at KPMG UK, I lead agile engineering teams to bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI research and real-world business execution. I am deeply passionate about the entire ML lifecycle—from prompt engineering and model fine-tuning (LoRA/QLoRA) to building robust LLMOps pipelines and enterprise guardrails. My goal is always to deliver intelligent solutions that are not only highly accurate but also secure, ethical, and optimized for performance. When I am not architecting AI systems, I enjoy exploring the latest advancements in machine learning, tackling complex data challenges, and mentoring junior engineers to build the next generation of AI solutions.
